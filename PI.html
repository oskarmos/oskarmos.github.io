A little about my actual identity, I am an artistic, creative, technological, and extroverted person. I am also brave and stupid, taking any task that will be thrown at me, and no matter how hard it will be, I’ll try my best. Kinda summarizes my life well, I tackle big projects and big ideas in life and then figure them out along the way.
Through this, I realized that I love learning, I set myself a goal of what I want to achieve, and I don’t care how hard it is to get to the goal, I’ll try to learn what I have to learn to get there. This doesn’t stop on projects though, random topics, self-care, health, reading philosophy, etc. I just like learning about things, but while I’m learning about things,
Self-discipline and confidence are things that are a big part of me nowadays, built through things like dopamine abstinence, and exercise, among others.
I’m also very adaptive and culturally developed, due to the fact that I’ve lived in different countries, making me always consider different factors when designing a product or service, oh and I know four languages.

I have a flaw, which is that my attention span and concentration are usually low, so unless I’m engaged in something, I struggle.
But because of this, my workflow is very active, if I’m developing a physical product, I’m going to prototype and make, rather than sit there and think, I’m a maker at heart whilst being mechanically inclined, I can also make actual working prototypes in a few hours.
Either that or sketching, a lot of sketching, as long as I keep myself busy and distracted with making, I can put out a lot of ideas from my head.
